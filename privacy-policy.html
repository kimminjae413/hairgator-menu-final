1. EXPLICIT STATEMENT: FACE DATA IS NOT RETAINED

Face data is not retained.

HAIRGATOR does NOT retain face data. Face images uploaded by users are processed transiently and deleted immediately after generating AI analysis results.   • Google Gemini API: To analyze facial features (face shape, proportions) and generate personalized hairstyle recommendations using Google's generative AI vision capabilities.
  • vModel API: To create realistic virtual hairstyle simulations by applying selected hairstyles to the user's photo using AI-based image synthesis technology.
